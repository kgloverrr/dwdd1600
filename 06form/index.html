About Us

Surf Boat Delivery Co. specializes in safe, reliable, and timely delivery of premium wakesurf and towboats to your home, marina, or storage location. Our team works directly with dealerships and manufacturers to ensure a seamless delivery experience from the moment your boat is sold to the moment it's in the water.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of boating enthusiasts, Surf Boat Delivery Co. emerged from the need to make surf boat ownership more convenient and accessible. We started with a single flatbed truck and a vision to provide white-glove delivery service to customers across the region.

Today, we've grown into a trusted delivery partner for many of the industry's top brands, including MasterCraft, Nautique, Malibu, Centurion, and Supra. Whether it's across town or across the state, our fleet of trailers and trucks ensures your boat arrives safely and on time.

What We Deliver

We focus exclusively on the delivery of wakesurf boats, towboats, and accessories. From dockside drop-offs to remote lake locations, we handle every logistical detail so you don’t have to.

Our services include delivery coordination, pre-arrival checks, scheduling with marinas, and even photo documentation of arrival. We treat every delivery like it’s our own boat.
