Major Surf Boat Companies

Industry Leaders

Several companies dominate the surf boat market by offering specialized features for wake surfing and wakeboarding:

Malibu Boats
Inventors of the Surf Gate™ system, Malibu is a top name in wake sports with innovative ballast systems and rider customization.
MasterCraft
Known for high-end boats, MasterCraft’s Gen 2 Surf System and premium finishes make it a favorite for competitive riders.
Centurion Boats
Centurion’s Opti-V hull design produces massive, customizable waves. It’s known for fuel-efficient yet powerful boats.
Nautique
Owned by Correct Craft, Nautique builds the iconic G-Series and Paragon boats, using the Nautique Surf System (NSS).
Tige and ATX
Tige created the Convex V hull and TAPS wake system. Their sister brand ATX offers simplified versions for budget-conscious riders.

Each company contributes unique designs and technologies that have advanced the surf boat industry significantly.
