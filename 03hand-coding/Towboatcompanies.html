Top Surf Boat Companies

Explore the brands shaping the world of wakesurf and wakeboard boats

Malibu Boats

Founded in 1982, Malibu Boats is a pioneer in the surf boat industry. Known for innovations like Surf Gate and the Power Wedge, Malibu creates customizable waves for wakesurfing and wakeboarding.

Website: www.malibuboats.com

Nautique

Nautique is known for premium craftsmanship and performance. Their G-Series boats are a favorite among professionals for delivering large, consistent wakes and luxurious interiors.

Website: www.nautique.com

MasterCraft

MasterCraft has been building high-quality towboats since 1968. Their models feature advanced ballast systems, touchscreen controls, and a reputation for innovation in the wakesurfing world.

Website: www.mastercraft.com

Centurion Boats

Centurion focuses specifically on surf performance, offering deep-V hulls and powerful wake systems. Their boats are a favorite for competitive riders looking for massive waves and precise control.

Website: www.centurionboats.com

Supra Boats

Supra offers a strong blend of performance and luxury. With bold designs, powerful engines, and smart tech, Supra boats are designed for serious riders and families alike.

Website: www.supraboats.com
