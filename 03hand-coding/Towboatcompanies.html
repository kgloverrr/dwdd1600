Major Surf Boat Companies

The surf boat industry has grown rapidly in the past two decades. Below are some of the most well-known companies that manufacture high-performance wake surfing boats.

Malibu Boats

Malibu is one of the pioneers in wake and surf boat innovation. Their patented Surf Gate™ technology allows riders to easily switch sides without moving ballast. Malibu also owns Axis Wake and has a wide range of boats tailored for every rider.

MasterCraft

Known for luxury and performance, MasterCraft offers the Gen 2 Surf System and the flagship X and XT series boats. Their models are packed with touchscreen controls, customizable wave profiles, and luxury features.

Centurion Boats

Centurion is known for its deep-V hulls and Opti-V design, which creates some of the best surf waves in the industry. Centurion also emphasizes fuel efficiency and rider adjustability.

Nautique

Nautique, part of Correct Craft, is the maker of the G-Series and Paragon models. Their Nautique Surf System (NSS) and integrated ballast help deliver championship-quality waves.
