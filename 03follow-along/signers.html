Who Were the Signers

Famous Singers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include

Thomas Jefferson (Virginia)
Primary author of the Declaration. Later became the 3rd President of the United States.
John Adams (Massachusetts)
Strong advocate for independence. Later became the 2nd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin (Pennsylvania)
Renowned inventor, diplomat, and statesman. Key figure in gaining French support during the Revolutionary War.
John Hancock (Massachusetts)
President of the Continental Congress. Known for his large and bold signatureâ€”"sign your John Hancock" comes from him.
Samuel Adams (Massachusetts)
Cousin of John Adams. Leader in the Sons of Liberty and a key revolutionary figure.

These men were not only important as signers but also played critical roles in the founding and early governance of the United States.
