The Evolution of Audi Cars

1909–1930s
Audi was founded by August Horch and quickly became known for innovation and competition success.
1960s–1980s
Audi launched the iconic Quattro and became part of Volkswagen Group.
1990s–2000s
Audi rose in prestige with the A-series and TT, blending technology and design.
2010s–Now
Audi leads in electric vehicles, with the e-tron line and cutting-edge driver tech.
